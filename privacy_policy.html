Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Welcome to CineNest ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application CineNest (package: com.findYourShow.moviedatabase).

By using the app, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

a) Information You Provide

  • Account registration: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored securely via Firebase Authentication).
  • Watchlist data: Movies, TV shows, seasons, and episodes you add to your watchlist are stored locally on your device.
  • Content preferences: Settings such as adult content filter (include/exclude) are saved locally.

b) Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage analytics: We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymised interaction events such as movie clicks, TV show clicks, and cast member clicks (content name and ID only).
  • Crash reports: We use Firebase Crashlytics to automatically collect crash and error reports to help us fix bugs. These reports may include device model, OS version, and app state at the time of the crash — no personally identifiable information is intentionally included.
  • Device information: General device and OS information collected by Firebase services (e.g., device model, OS version, language).

c) Third-Party Content Data

The app fetches movie and TV show information from the following third-party services. We do not share your personal data with these services; they are used only for displaying content metadata:

  • TMDB (The Movie Database) — movie and TV show details, images, cast info, ratings, and watch providers.
  • Trakt — community comments and discussions about movies and TV shows.
  • YouTube — trailers are played via the YouTube Android Player library.
  • TMDB Image CDN / YouTube Thumbnails — poster and thumbnail images.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the app's core features (discovery, watchlist, search).
  • To send you push notifications about entertainment content (via Firebase Cloud Messaging). You can opt out by disabling notifications in your device settings.
  • To understand how users interact with the app through aggregated, anonymised analytics.
  • To identify and fix crashes and errors through crash reports.
  • To authenticate you and manage your account securely.

3. Advertising

The app integrates Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) for displaying native ads. Ads are currently disabled in this version of the app. If ads are enabled in a future version, this policy will be updated, and Google's privacy policy will apply to ad-related data collection. You can review Google's privacy policy at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Data Storage

a) Local Storage (On Your Device)

  • Watchlist database: Your saved movies, TV shows, seasons, and episodes are stored in a local SQLite database (movies.db) on your device only.
  • App preferences: Login status, username, email, and content filter settings are stored in Encrypted SharedPreferences using AES-256 encryption.

b) Cloud Storage (Firebase)

  • Your name and email are stored in Firebase Firestore under your unique user ID.
  • Firebase Authentication securely manages your login credentials.
  • Firebase servers may be located outside your country. By using the app, you consent to the transfer of your data to Firebase's servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Firebase / Google: As described above, for authentication, analytics, crash reporting, and push notifications.
  • Legal obligations: If required by law or to protect our legal rights.

6. Push Notifications

We use Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to send you push notifications about entertainment content. When you install the app, you are subscribed to the "entertainment" topic by default. You can opt out at any time by disabling notifications for CineNest in your device's system settings.

7. Children's Privacy

CineNest is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permissions:

  • INTERNET — required to fetch movie/show data from TMDB, Trakt, and Firebase.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — to detect internet connectivity and show appropriate messages.
  • VIBRATE — to enable vibration for push notification alerts.

9. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all network communication.
  • AES-256 encrypted local storage for sensitive preferences.
  • Firebase Authentication for secure account management.
  • ProGuard code obfuscation in release builds.

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Delete your account and associated data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings.
  • Clear your local watchlist data by clearing the app's data in device settings.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
